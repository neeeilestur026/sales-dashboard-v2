Accounting Process Flow

Inventory → Quotation → Sales Order → Purchase Order (+ AP Aging) → Receiving → Invoice. Each step loads from the previous one.

Flow backend not configured.

Deploy apps-script/FlowAPI.gs as a web app and paste its /exec URL into dashboard/js/flow-api.js (FLOW_API_URL).

STEP 1
Inventory
Items with purchase price, shipping & landed cost. Qty may be zero.
STEP 2
Quotation
Load items from inventory; set quoted qty & price.
STEP 3
Sales Order
Load a quotation; edit quantities & remove items.
STEP 4
Purchase Order
Load a sales order; supplier price & currency. Creates AP entry.
STEP 4b
AP Aging
Edit the PHP amount, due date & payment status.
STEP 5
Materials Receiving
Load a PO; add shipping → landed cost → inventory.
STEP 6
Invoice / Issuance
Load a sales order; deduct inventory; record COGS.
LEDGER
General Ledger
Journal entries auto-posted at each step, trial balance & chart of accounts.
ACCOUNTING
Accounting (per Sales Order)
Read-only full detail of each sales order across the whole flow — quotation to invoice.
