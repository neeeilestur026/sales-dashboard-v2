New Payment Request (Other Payables)

For expenses and other payables (not purchase orders). Approval: Accounting → then Management & Director. Generates the same PRF document as the old system.

A Draft or Rejected request can be edited directly. Once it has been submitted or approved, use Revise on the row — that returns it to Draft and clears every approval, so the corrected amount, payee or bank account must be approved again before payment. The PDF is regenerated automatically on save.
